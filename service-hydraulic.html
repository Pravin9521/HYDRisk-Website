Advanced Hydraulic Analysis

Our hydraulic modeling services provide detailed analysis of water flow behavior in complex systems, helping you understand and predict flood patterns.

1D Hydraulic Modeling

Cross-section based modeling for rivers, channels, and pipe networks. Ideal for long river reaches and complex hydraulic structures.

  • Steady and unsteady flow analysis
  • Bridge and culvert modeling
  • Floodplain mapping
  • Sediment transport

2D Hydraulic Modeling

Grid-based modeling for complex flow patterns, urban flooding, and overland flow analysis.

  • Urban flood modeling
  • Dam break analysis
  • Coastal flooding
  • Storm water management

Coupled 1D/2D Modeling

Integrated modeling combining 1D channel flow with 2D overland flow for comprehensive flood analysis.

  • River-floodplain interaction
  • Urban drainage systems
  • Levee breach analysis
  • Real-time forecasting
