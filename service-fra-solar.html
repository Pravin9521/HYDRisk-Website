Flood Risk Assessments for Solar PV Plants Global

Flooding can severely impact the performance and safety of solar PV installations. Our global service provides location-specific flood risk analysis to guide placement, design, and mitigation strategies for solar farms.

Solar PV Plant with Flood Risk Analysis

What it is:

Solar PV plants often cover large areas, making them susceptible to widespread flood damage. Our assessments for solar plants specifically analyze the unique vulnerabilities of these installations, such as panel submergence, foundation erosion, and damage to electrical infrastructure. We use advanced mapping and hydrological data to pinpoint areas at risk and recommend protective measures.

Why it matters:

  • Protecting Investment: Solar PV plants are significant investments. An FRA helps safeguard against costly flood damage to panels, inverters, and supporting structures.
  • Operational Continuity: Minimizes downtime and ensures the continuous generation of power, which is critical for revenue and energy supply.
  • Compliance: Ensures the plant meets regulatory requirements for flood risk management.

We Offer:

  • Site-specific flood zone analysis
  • Rainfall-runoff modeling
  • Historical flood data integration
  • Worst case scenario Analysis (RP -200, RP- 100, .. RP-2)
  • Risk mapping & mitigation recommendations
  • Post flood modelling with adapted Mitigation
